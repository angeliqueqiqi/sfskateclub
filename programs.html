Saturday Skate Program

Ages 8-13. Visit and skate 2 different skate parks every Saturday for four Saturdays; receive small group lessons with a sponsored skateboarder.

Eduskate

Visit our Club Hub and participate in group activities under our community after school program.

Youth Summer Skate Camp

Ages 8-13. Tour and skate Bay Area skateparks and receive small group lessons with a sponsored skateboarder.

Teen Summer Camp

Ages 12-15. Tour and skate Bay Area skateparks and receive small group lessons with a sponsored skateboarder.

Private Skate Lessons

Private one-on-one lessons with an SF Skate Club instructor. Have the chance to learn or improve on your skateboarding skills.

Parties and Events

Skate games, group lessons, prizes and recorded footage; nothing goes together better than a little cake, all your friends and tons of skateboarding.
